介绍 Introduction

I am going to detail here my personal journey through Gestalt Theory. It took me around fifteen years to get a proper look at this topic. Gestalt is not a term you will hear a lot in a studio. Actually you never hear it in the lighting department.

我将在这里详细介绍我个人对格式塔理论的了解。我花了大约十五年的时间才对这个主题有了正确的认识。格式塔不是你在工作室经常听到的一个术语。实际上，你在灯光部门永远不会听到它。

Unfortunately I do not have many personal lighting examples about Gestalt. This theory is more about composition anyway. Artists working in layout and set dressing would certainly know better. But my job is very dependent of the framing of a shot. And knowing about these principles can be very helpful.

不幸的是，我没有很多关于格式塔的个人照明示例。无论如何，这个理论更多的是关于构图。从事布局和布景工作的艺术家肯定会更了解。但我的工作非常依赖于镜头的构图。了解这些原则会非常有帮助。

学校里的格式塔 Gestalt at school

Back in 2006, I had a teacher at Supinfocom, called ​Marc Bigeast​, who was very interested by Gestalt Theory. During my two years of study with him, he mentioned the concept a lot. He mainly described it as​a theory of the shapes​.

2006 年，我在Supinfocom有一位老师，叫Marc Bigeast ，他对​格式塔理论非常感兴趣。在我跟随他学习的两年里，他多次提到这个概念。他主要把它描述为​一种形状理论​。

Back then I did not realize its importance because I was so worried about specular values or if I should use a Blinn or a Phong material. And the theory seemed a bit old-fashioned. As students we would much more focus on displacement maps and area lights. Mostly technical stuff.

那时我还没有意识到它的重要性，因为我太担心镜面反射值，或者我是否应该使用 Blinn 或 Phong 材质。而且这个理论似乎有点过时。作为学生，我们会更加关注位移贴图和区域光。主要是技术方面的东西。

​

As a student this example can be unsettling.

作为一名学生，这个例子可能会令人不安。

To be honest, I was discovering Maya at the same time and I felt so overwhelmed by the amount of data to process that I kinda lost track of ​my main purpose : making great pictures​. Anyway, after graduating, I forgot about Gestalt and went on with my career.

说实话，当时我正在研究玛雅，处理的数据量让我应接不暇，我有点忘记了​自己的主要目的：拍出好照片​。无论如何，毕业后，我忘记了格式塔，继续我的职业生涯。

If only I could have connected the dots, I would have realized that this Pixar short film was using Gestalt Theory on almost every shot.

如果我能将这些点联系起来，我就会意识到这部皮克斯短片几乎在每一个镜头中都运用了格式塔理论。

Wikipedia uses the term “Gestalt Psychology” probably because the Gestalt principles exposed in the early 20th century were then taken up by psychoanalysts and theorized into a new form of therapy (the Gestalt Therapy).

维基百科使用“格式塔心理学”这一术语，可能是因为 20 世纪早期揭示的格式塔原理随后被精神分析学家采纳并理论化为一种新的治疗形式（格式塔疗法）。

That is basically the same concept. A more accurate term overall would be “principles of perceptual organization.”

这基本上是同一个概念。总体而言，更准确的术语应该是“感知组织原则”。

格式塔和皮克斯 Gestalt and Pixar

Thirteen years later, I was doing some research for my book and I found about the Pixel Cinematography course. It is a note from the 1996 SIGGRAPH session where Sharon Calahan talks about lighting principles : “​Cool ! Let’s see what Pixar had to say about Cinematography back in 1996 ! ​”

十三年后，我在为我的书做一些研究时，发现了像素电影摄影课程。这是 1996 年​SIGGRAPH会议上的一篇笔记，当时**Sharon Calahan**谈到了照明原理：“太酷了！让我们看看皮克斯在 1996 年对电影摄影有何看法！ ”

I guess you already know what is the main topic of this course : Gestalt Theory ! I could not believe my eyes ! Pixar talking about Gestalt Theory. Exactly like my teacher back at school. Crazy.

我想你已经知道这门课的主题是什么了：格式塔理论！ 我简直不敢相信自己的眼睛！皮克斯谈论格式塔理论。和我上学时的老师一模一样。太疯狂了。

This Siggraph talk was done four years before the short film ​For the birds​. It is pretty clear that all these concepts were used massively at Pixar.

这次 Siggraph 演讲是在短片For the bird上映四年前进行的。很明显，所有这些概念都在皮克斯得到了广泛应用。

更多格式塔联系 More Gestalt connections

Funnily enough, I recently realized that Craig Welsh also mentioned this course in his blog in 2014 :

有趣的是，我最近意识到 Craig Welsh 在 2014 年也在他的博客中提到过这门课程：

In the interview reference is made to Ms Calahan’s contribution to the 1996 SIGGRAPH session “Pixel Cinematography”. The course notes are available online here and are as pertinent today as they were in 1996.

采访中提到了 Calahan 女士对 1996 年SIGGRAPH会议“像素电影摄影”的贡献。课程笔记可在此处在线获取，并且与 1996 年一样具有现实意义。

I decided to read the course and see what would come out of it. Obviously all the technical parts are completely outdated. Back in 1996, there was no GI, so you had to fake most of the lighting. So all this stuff is pretty irrelevant.

我决定阅读课程内容，看看会有什么收获。显然，所有技术部分都已经过时了。1996年还没有 GI，所以你必须伪造大部分灯光。所以所有这些东西都无关紧要。

But all the Gestalt Principles described by Ms Calahan are still valid. There is one thing missing though : examples from movies, comics or paintings to illustrate her notes. I guess that during the presentation there were more images, but unfortunately they are not many available in the pdf.

但 Calahan 女士描述的所有格式塔原则仍然有效。 不过，有一件事缺失了：电影、漫画或绘画中的例子来说明她的笔记。我猜在演示过程中会有更多图片，但不幸的是，pdf 中可用的图片并不多。

So in this chapter I am going to read through this course, list the Gestalt principles and add some movie examples to make them even clearer.

因此在本章中，我将通读本课程，列出格式塔原则并添加一些电影示例以使它们更加清晰。

The Siggraph 2019 course from Dave Walvoord mentions some concepts clearly coming from Gestalt… But without mentioning even once the name of the theory itself. Many artists actually do not know about its existence.

Dave Walvoord的 Siggraph 2019 课程提到了一些显然来自格式塔的概念……但一次也没有提到该理论本身的名称。许多艺术家实际上并不知道它的存在。

通过灯光讲述故事 Storytelling through lighting

卡拉罕简介 Calahan Introduction

The part from Sharon Calahan starts in a great way :

Sharon Calahan 的部分开始得非常棒：

Although much of the content of this course is not solely applicable to lighting on the computer, its special needs are always in mind.

虽然本课程的大部分内容并不仅适用于计算机照明，但我们始终考虑到它的特殊需求。

Yes ! CG do have special needs that have to be accounted for.

是的！CG 确实有需要考虑到的特殊需求。

It follows like this :

如下所示：

Visual storytelling is a vast topic that reaches far beyond the realm of lighting. Most of it is not noticeable on a conscious level to the viewer, but adds depth and richness to the story and the visual experience.

视觉叙事是一个广泛的话题，远远超出了灯光的范畴。大部分视觉叙事在观众的意识层面上是无法察觉的，但却能为故事和视觉体验增添深度和丰富性。

Yes ! Gestalt theory is not exactly about lighting. It is mostly about composition.

是的！格式塔理论并不完全是关于照明的。它主要是关于构图的。

My teachers have always told me : composition is the most difficult part of an image. The rest should flow naturally. In a movie production, we are so camera dependent : animation, lighting, set dressing… We rely completely on the framing.

我的老师总是告诉我：构图是一幅图像中最难的部分。其余部分应该自然流畅。在电影制作中，我们非常依赖摄像机：动画、灯光、布景……我们完全依赖构图。

富有远见的演讲 Visionary talk

I just love how back in 1996 Sharon Calahan had already understood so many concepts :

我非常喜欢 Sharon Calahan 早在 1996 年就已经理解了这么多概念：

The commonality between live-action lighting and computer lighting is chiefly the thought process. […] in this discussion of lighting for synthetic cinema we will find that many live-action concepts apply.

实景照明和计算机照明之间的共同点主要在于思维过程。[…] 在关于合成电影照明的讨论中，我们会发现许多实景概念都适用。

It is really a visionary talk. I mean understanding all these connections before PBR is just brilliant. Then there is this beautiful sentence that makes so much sense :

这真是一次富有远见的演讲。我的意思是，在 PBR 之前理解所有这些联系真是太棒了。然后有一句非常有意义的漂亮句子：

It should be kept in mind that the sooner in the production process lighting can be designed, the more involved it can be in the storytelling process.

应该牢记的是，在制作过程中越早设计灯光，它在故事叙述过程中的参与度就越高。

Very impressive. That is exactly what we did on Playmobil since we were lighting Rough Layout (RLO). And that is pretty much what Pixar did on ​Toy Story 4​. So much ahead of her time !

非常令人印象深刻。这正是我们在Playmobil上所做的，因为我们在为粗略布局 (RLO) 打光。这几乎就是皮克斯在 《玩具总动员 4》 上所做的。远远领先于她的时代！

[…] it is important to understand the story-point behind each shot, and how it relates to the story as a whole. It is not enough the lighting designer simply illuminates the scene so the viewer can see what is happening, or to make it look pretty.

[…] 了解每个镜头背后的故事要点以及它与整个故事的关系非常重要。灯光设计师仅仅照亮场景以便观众可以看到正在发生的事情或使其看起来很漂亮是不够的。

I agree with this statement. Unfortunately in many productions, pretty is enough.

我同意这个说法。不幸的是，在很多作品中，漂亮就足够了。

Sharon Calahan was also very clever by focusing her talk on Gestalt Theory. These are timeless principles. She probably knew her talk would still be valid 30 years later. Smart move !

Sharon Calahan 的演讲也非常聪明，重点介绍了格式塔理论。这些是永恒的原则。 她可能知道 30 年后她的演讲仍然有效。聪明之举！

什么是格式塔？ What is Gestalt ?

This article explains pretty well the Gestalt principles. I would rather not copy/paste it. Please have a look !

这篇文章很好地解释了格式塔原理。我不想复制/粘贴它。请看一下！

In the 1920s, a group of cognitive psychologists in Germany developed a series of theories of visual perception known as the Gestalt Principles. There is no perfect English translation of the term “gestalt”. But we can interpret it as “whole”, ”pattern”, ”structure”, ”configuration” or “organized unity”. […]

20 世纪 20 年代，德国的一群认知心理学家提出了一系列视觉感知理论，被称为格式塔原理。“格式塔”一词没有完美的英文翻译。但我们可以将其解释为“整体”、“模式”、“结构”、“配置”或“有组织的统一体”。[…]

Their work on the perception of the whole rather than the parts from an aesthetic point of view resulted in the Gestalt Theory.

他们从美学角度对整体而非部分的感知的研究产生了格式塔理论。

These laws explain our perceptual tendencies and describe how humans typically see objects by grouping similar elements, recognizing patterns and simplifying complex images.

这些定律解释了我们的感知倾向，并描述了人类通常如何通过分组相似元素、识别模式和简化复杂图像来观察物体。

Artists use these principles to engage viewers via powerful -yet natural- “tricks” of composition and best practice design standards.

艺术家运用这些原则，通过强大而自然的构图“技巧”和最佳实践设计标准来吸引观众。

When you type Gestalt Theory in your search engine, you will find mostly articles about web design. But it totally applies to any kind of image really.

当你在搜索引擎中输入格式塔理论时，你会发现大部分文章都是关于网页设计的。但它实际上完全适用于任何类型的图像。

格式塔原则 Gestalt principles

We are now going to dive deep in the Gestalt topic. I could probably copy/paste the whole talk honestly. It is just great the way it is. Let’s begin !

我们现在要深入探讨格式塔主题。老实说，我可能会复制/粘贴整个演讲。它现在的样子就很棒。让我们开始吧！

The primary objective of good lighting is to show the viewer where to look. Shots are often on-screen only briefly, which means the storytelling effectiveness of a shot often depends upon how well, and how quickly, the viewer’s eye is led to the key story elements. […] Composition is a term which is used to collectively describe a group of related visual principles. […] They are not rules to be followed, but define a structure by which to explore creative possibilities. […] It can also help rescue a less-than-perfect composition.

良好灯光的主要目的是告诉观众应该看哪里。 镜头通常只在屏幕上短暂出现，这意味着镜头的叙事效果通常取决于观众的视线被引导到关键故事元素的速度和质量。[…] 构图是一个术语，用于集体描述一组相关的视觉原则。[…]它们不是要遵循的规则，而是定义了一种探索创意可能性的结构。 […] 它还可以帮助挽救不完美的构图。

There are plenty of websites that describe the Gestalt principles. We will try to describe them one by one.

有很多网站描述了格式塔原则。我们将尝试逐一描述它们。

It is true that websites about Gestalt mostly talk about 2d examples such as motion design or logos. But I believe a proper study between cinematography and Gestalt is possible.

确实，关于格式塔的网站大多讨论 2d 示例，例如动态设计或徽标。但我相信电影摄影和格式塔之间的适当研究是可能的。

相似原理 Similarity principle

Similar objects will be counted as the same group and this technique can be used to draw a viewer’s attention.

相似的物体将被视为同一组，这种技术可用于吸引观看者的注意力。

​

​

There is a great example of this principle in Piper. All the characters are adult sandpipers except the protagonist who’s a baby. The viewer will see the adult sandpipers as a group rather than individuals and will therefore focus more on the main character.

《Piper》 很好地体现了这一原则。除了主角还是个婴儿之外，其他所有角色都是成年鹬。观众会将成年鹬视为一个群体，而不是个体，因此会将注意力更多地集中在主角身上。

In lighting this principle would be ​grouping with light and shadow​. This process is described (once again !) by ​Dave Walvoord​.

在照明中，这一原则将与光和影相结合。Dave​Walvoord再次描述了这一过程。

​

​

Even if Gestalt is not explicitly mentioned here, it is clearly the topic.

即使这里没有明确提到格式塔，但它显然是主题。

[…] the very large tree behind Hiccup that could easily dominate the frame is flagged into shadow. Great liberty can be taken with the placement of shadows without the audience becoming aware. Shadows generated by off camera objects, such as a cloud in this example, are easily justified regardless of whether or not a cloud exists in the correct location to cast the shadow. […] the audience rarely notices when a shadow in one shot is not present in the following shot as long as the camera is not in the same location. This gives the Lighting Designer great liberty to ignore the continuity of lighting throughout the scene.

[…] 希卡普身后那棵大树很容易占据整个画面，但被标记为阴影。阴影的放置可以非常自由，观众却不会察觉。镜头外物体产生的阴影（例如本例中的云）很容易被证明是合理的，无论云是否存在于投射阴影的正确位置。 […] 只要摄像机不在同一位置，观众很少会注意到一个镜头中的阴影在下一个镜头中不存在。这让灯光设计师可以非常自由地忽略整个场景中灯光的连续性。

简单原则（Pragnanz 或 Good Figure） Simplicity principle (Pragnanz or Good Figure)

I have merged both laws (simplicity and Pragnanz) since they are quite similar.

我合并了这两条定律（简单性和 Pragnanz），因为它们非常相似。

A stimulus will be organized into as good a figure as possible. […] When learners are presented with visuals, there is an unconscious effort to simplify what is perceived into what the viewer can understand.

刺激物将被组织成尽可能好的图形。[…] 当向学习者展示视觉效果时，他们会无意识地努力将所感知到的内容简化为观看者可以理解的内容。

在照明方面，清晰、简单地解读镜头非常重要。我们不希望观众猜测这是光还是影。Dave Walvoord 的论文中有一个完美的例子。

I have never heard the word Gestalt in any of the studios I have worked at.

在我工作过的任何工作室里，我从未听说过格式塔这个词。

Dragon lighting likes to keep a clear delineation between light and shadow. […] the shadow area is now deliberately being used to frame Grimmel and draw attention to him.

龙灯喜欢在光与影之间保持清晰的界限。[…] 现在故意使用阴影区域来框住格里梅尔并吸引人们对他的注意。

Very often the foreground area of a shot will be in shadow. It guides the eye.

镜头的前景区域经常会处于阴影中。阴影会引导视线。

就近原则 Proximity principle

The law of proximity states that items placed near each other appear to be a group. […] The brain tends to group objects that are close to each other.

接近定律指出，彼此靠近放置的物品看起来是一组。[…] 大脑倾向于将彼此靠近的物体归为一组。

​

​

​

Same here in Up (Director : ​Pete Docter​). The helium balloons are seen as one group.

同样在 《飞屋环游记》 （导演：皮特·多克特）中。氦气球被视为一个整体。

延续原则 Continuation principle

Continuation is the eye’s instinctive action to follow a direction derived from the visual field.

延续是眼睛追随视野方向的本能动作。

图形与背景原则 Figure & Ground principle

Negative or empty spaces will likewise be organized and grouped. […] For example, two different foreground colors let the viewer perceive different things from the same illustration.

负空间或空白空间同样也会被组织和分组。[…]例如，两种不同的前景色让观看者从同一张插图中感知到不同的东西。

Minimalist movie posters are the perfect example for Gestalt principles. Because they need to use very few elements to make a clear statement. No extra detail, straight to the essential. Figure & Ground is the equivalent of positive and negative space.

极简主义 电影海报是格式塔原则的完美范例。因为它们 只需要使用很少的元素就能表达清楚。没有多余的细节，直奔主题。图形和背景相当于正空间和负空间。

封闭原则 Closure principle

Patterns or objects that continue in one direction, even if interrupted by another pattern, are perceived as being continuous. […] Our minds will tend to close gaps and complete unfinished forms.

图案或物体如果在一个方向上连续，即使被另一个图案打断，也会被视为连续的。[…] 我们的思想会倾向于填补空白，完成未完成的形式。

​

​

​

Animated movies are such a perfect example of Gestalt principles.

动画电影是格式塔原理的完美例子。

团结和谐原则 Unity & Harmony principle

Unity implies that a congruity or arrangement exists among the elements in a design; they look as through they belong together, as though there is some visual connection beyond mere chance that has caused them to come together. […] The elements of the composition appear to belong together, relate to each other, and to otherwise visually agree.

统一性意味着设计中的元素之间存在一致性或排列；它们看起来就像是属于一起的，好像存在着某种视觉联系，而不仅仅是偶然的，才使它们结合在一起。[…] 构图中的元素看起来是属于一起的，相互关联，并且在视觉上是一致的。

共同命运原则 Common Fate principle

Humans perceive visual elements that move in the same speed and/or direction as parts of a single stimulus. A common example of this is a flock of birds.

人类将以相同速度和/或方向移动的视觉元素视为单一刺激的一部分。一个常见的例子是一群鸟。

The short film Changes (Director : ​**Daniel Martinez Lara**​) is a great example of Gestalt Theory. It uses all kinds of Gestalt principles : proximity, similarity, common fate, isolation and even motion.

短片《Changes》 （导演：丹尼尔·马丁内斯·拉拉）是格式塔理论的一个很好的例子。它运用了各种格式塔原则：接近性、相似性、共同命运、孤立甚至运动。

其他原则 Other principles

There are so many laws that it would be very difficult to describe them all. I’ll just give you a couple more :

法律太多了，很难一一描述。我再举几个例子：

  • The law of​common region​or enclosure. Elements tend to be perceived into groups if they are sharing an area with a clearly defined boundary.
  • 共同区域​或封闭性法则。 如果元素共享一个具有明确边界的区域，则它们往往会被划分为多个组。
  • The law of​​connection​ . All images do not have the same meaning to us because we interpret their meanings based on our experiences.
  • 连接​定律。 并非所有图像对于我们都有相同的意义，因为我们会根据自己的经验来解读它们的意义。
  • The law of​​isomorphic correspondence​ . The brain attempts to interpret the world by finding constancy. Interesting stuff.
  • 同构​对应定律。 大脑试图通过寻找恒定性来解释世界。很有趣的东西。

Are you familiar with the work of Lou Romano ? He is an amazing concept artist using the Gestalt principles in most of his work.

你熟悉Lou Romano的作品吗？他是一位出色的概念艺术家，在大部分作品中都运用了格式塔原理。

​

​

​

​

Now that we have seen what the Gestalt principles are, we are going to focus on its two most important applications : Emphasis and Balance.

现在我们已经了解了格式塔原理是什么，我们将重点关注它的两个最重要的应用：强调和平衡。

重点（焦点原则） Emphasis (Principle of focal point)

Every visual presentation needs a focal point, called the center of interest or point of emphasis.

每个视觉呈现都需要一个焦点，称为兴趣中心或重点点。

An image without emphasis is like wallpaper, the eye has no particular place to look and no reward for having tried. […] A composition may have more than one focal point, but one should dominate.

没有重点的图像就像墙纸，眼睛没有特定的位置可看，即使尝试了也没有回报。 […] 构图可能有多个焦点，但应该有一个焦点占主导地位。

强调对比 Contrast emphasis

Contrast can be achieved with shape, size, color, texture, brightness or even motion. A focal point results when one element differs significantly from other elements. […] Unique or minority elements within larger groups tend to attract our attention.

对比可以通过形状、大小、颜色、纹理、亮度甚至运动来实现。当一个元素与其他元素有显著差异时，就会产生焦点。[…] 较大群体中的独特或少数元素往往会吸引我们的注意力。

We’ll see more examples about contrast in this chapter.

在本章中我们将看到更多关于对比的例子。

​

The character of Eve in Wall-e is a great example of contrast through shape, color and texture in the first part of the movie. She looks so different from everything else.

《机器人总动员》中的夏娃角色是电影第一部分中通过形状、颜色和纹理形成对比的一个很好的例子。她看起来与其他一切都不同。

切线强调 Tangent emphasis

Tangents, where two edges just touch each other, can produce a strong point of emphasis by creating visual tension.

切线，即两个边缘刚好接触的地方，可以通过产生视觉张力来产生强烈的强调点。

I thought this to be very interesting because 90% of the time you want to avoid tangents. They really have a bad reputation in the industry. Here is an example from There will be blood (Director : Paul Thomas Anderson). I actually copied it from this blog.

我认为这很有趣，因为90% 的时间你都想避免离题。他们在业内确实名声不好。这是《血色将至》（导演：保罗·托马斯·安德森）中的一个例子。我实际上是从这个博客上抄袭来的。

There Will Be Blood (2007). The coerced baptism and humiliation of Plainview as he is forced to admit publicly his sins. A blindingly white cross dominates the frame, centrally located and positioned to literally rest upon the head of the kneeling Plainview (played by Daniel Day-Lewis). Never mind avoiding those tangents !

血色将至​(2007)。普莱恩维尤被迫接受洗礼，并被迫公开承认自己的罪行，遭受羞辱。一个耀眼的白色十字架占据了画面的主导地位，位于画面中央，正好位于跪着的普莱恩维尤（丹尼尔·戴-刘易斯饰演）的头上。别介意避开这些离题的话！

​

​

We generally try to avoid tangent contacts unless for obvious composition reasons.

我们通常会尽量避免切线接触，除非出于明显的构图原因。

With care, tangents can be created intentionally to attract viewer interest; however, most of the time they are accidental and distracting. So be careful when using them !

只要小心，就可以故意制造离题来吸引观众的兴趣；然而，大多数时候，离题都是无意的，会分散注意力。 所以使用离题时一定要小心！

隔离重点 Isolation emphasis

When object defies grouping, by not being near or similar to any other object, it calls attention to itself and becomes a point of emphasis through tension.

当物体无法分组时，由于不靠近或不与其他任何物体相似，它会引起对自身的注意，并通过张力成为强调点。

​

​

The lonely figure facing a group. Classic.

孤独的身影面对着一群人。经典。

角度强调 Angle emphasis

[…] Using perspective angles and other edges […] lead the eye to the focal point.

[…] 使用透视角和其他边缘 […] 将眼睛引导至焦点。

Our eye goes straight at it. Why do we see so often this kind of composition, from left to right ? My guess is that in western countries we read from the left to the right.

下面的镜头来自 《快乐的大脚 2》 （导演：乔治·米勒），其构图类似。左侧的前景元素看着右侧的背景元素。我们的视线直视它。为什么我们经常看到这种从左到右的构图？我猜是因为在西方国家，我们是从左到右阅读的。

I just love how the krill swarm turns into a skull.

我真的很喜欢磷虾群变成头骨的样子。

强调形状 Shape emphasis

The brain tends to characterize shape as either rectilinear or curvilinear. […] By creating an image with primarily one type, the other type becomes a point of emphasis.

大脑倾向于将形状描述为直线或曲线。[…] 通过主要以一种类型创建图像，另一种类型成为重点。

​

​

​

The Gestalt theory says that an angle (hard shape) will catch more our attention than a circle (soft shape). In a render, the angle of a window can possibly be an issue as many times it catches your attention.

格式塔理论认为，角度（硬形状）比圆形（软形状）更能吸引我们的注意力。在渲染中，窗户的角度可能是一个问题，因为它多次引起你的注意。

认可重点 Recognition emphasis

Because of the human need for self-recognition, human or anthropomorphic characters will naturally attract more attention than inanimate objects.

由于人类需要自我认知，人类或拟人化的角色自然会比无生命的物体吸引更多的注意力。

运动强调 Motion emphasis

If all objects are moving except one, the eye will be drawn to the one which is not moving. The opposite case, of only object moving, is more common and even more effective in attracting attention.

如果除了一个物体之外所有物体都在移动，那么眼睛就会被吸引到不动的物体上。相反的情况，即只有物体在移动，则更常见，也更能吸引注意力。

I thought the following shots fromHappy Feet 2 (Director : ​George Miller​) would be a very good example. Bill and Will have the same designs and colors than their peers but they are swimming against them. Which make them different and create emphasis.

我认为《快乐的大脚 2》 （导演：乔治·米勒）中的以下镜头是一个很好的例子。比尔和威尔的设计和颜色与他们的同龄人相同，但他们却在与他们对抗。这让他们与众不同，并产生了强调。

Matt Damon and Brad Pitt as krill. Just brilliant !

马特·达蒙和布拉德·皮特饰演磷虾。太精彩了！

平衡 Balance

When an object is unbalanced, it looks as though it will topple over. […] An entire image which is off-balance will make the viewer uncomfortable because he wants to balance it, but cannot. This discomfort can be desirable if it enhances the mood or story point.

当物体失去平衡时，它看起来好像会倒塌。[…] 整个画面失去平衡会让观看者感到不舒服，因为他想保持平衡，但做不到。如果这种不适能增强情绪或故事情节，那么这种不适就是可取的。

Off-balance camera is called Dutch angle.

离平衡的摄影机称为荷兰角。

荷兰角 Dutch Angle

我想到的第一个例子是 《七宗罪》 （导演：大卫·芬奇，摄影指导：达吕斯·康吉）。当约翰·多伊用枪指着侦探米尔斯（布拉德·皮特）的头时，镜头被扭曲，让观众感到不舒服。分析得很好。

对称性：水平平衡 Symmetry : Horizontal balance

I found the following section very interesting.

我发现以下部分非常有趣。

Visual balance is achieved using two equations. The first balances the image around a horizontal axis, where the two halves, top and bottom should achieve a sense of equilibrium. […] Where these horizontal divisions are, relative to where we are, tell us how tall we are, how far off the ground we might be, […].

视觉平衡是通过两个方程实现的。第一个方程围绕水平轴平衡图像，其中顶部和底部两半应达到平衡感。[…] 相对于我们所在的位置，这些水平划分的位置告诉我们我们有多高，我们离地面有多远，[…]。

Here comes the best part in my opinion.

我认为这是最好的部分。

[…] the placement of a character within the image format and the angle that the camera sees him can imply the height of a character. And since we tend to associate height as a dominating physical characteristic, it can say something about the importance of the character in his current situation.

[…] 角色在图像格式中的位置以及摄像机拍摄他的角度可以暗示角色的身高。由于我们倾向于将身高视为主要的身体特征，因此它可以说明角色在当前情况下的重要性。

对称性：垂直平衡 Symmetry : Vertical balance

The second equation of visual balance divides the image around a central vertical axis. The horizontal format of cinema is most affected by this left / right ratio. […] A visual object will appear as incomplete if the visual object is not balanced or symmetrical. […] Heavily used in architecture, symmetrical balance feels very formal, permanent, strong, calm and stable.

视觉平衡的第二个方程式围绕中心垂直轴划分图像。电影的水平格式受左右比例的影响最大。 […] 如果视觉对象不平衡或不对称，则视觉对象会显得不完整。[…] 对称平衡在建筑中被广泛使用，给人一种非常正式、永久、强大、平静和稳定的感觉。

With two similar sides, there is an obvious visual importance to whatever element is placed on the center axis.

由于两条边相似，因此放置在中心轴上的任何元素都具有明显的视觉重要性。

不对称平衡 Asymmetrical Balance

A scale is balanced by putting equal weight on both sides. […] The balance of a composition is similar except that visual interest becomes the unit of measure. Visual interest comes in many shapes, sizes, values, colors and textures, each with varying density.

天平的平衡是通过在两边施加相等的重量来实现的。[…] 构图的平衡与之类似，只是视觉趣味成为衡量单位。视觉趣味有多种形状、大小、价值、颜色和纹理，每种都有不同的密度。

作品 Composition

I really want to state this here : composition is key in a shot. I really think that a good framing is more important than a good lighting. Actually I would even say that lighting is probably the less important task in a movie. If I had to choose, I would rather have a good story and a so-so lighting than the other way around !

我真的想在这里说：构图是镜头的关键。 我真的认为好的构图比好的灯光更重要。实际上，我甚至会说灯光在电影中可能是不太重要的任务。 如果必须选择的话，我宁愿有一个好的故事和一个马马虎虎的灯光，而不是相反！

虽然格式塔理论是在1910年左右形成的，但显然它在此之前就已经存在了。有人甚至说这些原则已经应用于史前艺术。
